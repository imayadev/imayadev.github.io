LabNotes is a student-led science platform created to make science more accessible, engaging, and relevant for young people. This collaborative journal brings together articles, artwork, and creative science communication from students who are curious about the world and passionate about understanding how it works. Our aim is to encourage exploration beyond the classroom, build confidence in communicating ideas, and create a space where young people can share work they are genuinely proud of.

Whether it is space, chemistry, biology, animals, engineering or innovation, LabNotes shares science in a way that is thoughtful, engaging, and easy to explore.

LabNotes exists to support students in:

  • exploring science beyond the school curriculum
  • developing clear and confident communication
  • connecting creativity with scientific understanding
    • sharing their work with a real audience
